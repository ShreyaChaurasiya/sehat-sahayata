About Us

We provide healthcare support and guidance.

Sehat Sahayata is a healthcare support platform designed to assist patients and families by providing guidance, quick help, and access to essential healthcare services. Our mission is to ensure that no one feels helpless when facing health issues.

We understand that finding the right medical support at the right time can be challenging. That’s why Sehat Sahayata acts as a bridge between patients and healthcare professionals, making the process simple, fast, and accessible.

Our platform offers features like AI-powered chatbot assistance, easy appointment booking, and community support to guide users in making informed health decisions.
